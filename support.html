Frequently Asked Questions

How does Seth work?

Simply speak or type in Arabic, and Seth will translate it to natural English with pronunciation guides in Arabic script so you know exactly how to say it.

Which Arabic dialects are supported?

Seth understands Syrian, Lebanese, Egyptian, Gulf, and Iraqi dialects, as well as Modern Standard Arabic.

What is Conversation Mode?

In Conversation Mode, you can record the other person's English response and Seth will translate it back to Arabic for you in real-time.

Is my data private?

Yes! We don't store your translations or voice recordings. See our Privacy Policy for details.
