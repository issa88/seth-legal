Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Seth ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Arabic-to-English translation app.

2. Information We Collect

Device Information: We generate an anonymous device identifier to track your usage limits and subscription tier. This ID is randomly generated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics including:

  • Number of translations per day
  • Number of conversations per day
  • Selected dialect and accent preferences
  • Subscription tier

Voice Data: When you use voice input, your audio is processed by third-party services (OpenAI Whisper) for transcription. We do not store your voice recordings.

Feedback: If you submit feedback through the app, we collect your message along with your device ID and usage context to help improve the app.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name or personal identity
  • Your email address or phone number
  • Your location data
  • Your contacts or photos
  • Your translation content (processed in real-time, not stored)

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide translation and text-to-speech services
  • To enforce daily usage limits based on your subscription
  • To improve our app and services
  • To respond to your feedback

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude: For AI-powered translations
  • OpenAI Whisper: For speech-to-text transcription
  • Google Cloud Text-to-Speech: For voice synthesis

These services process your data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS.

7. Data Retention

Usage data is retained only for the current billing period. Feedback is retained until resolved or deleted upon request.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: sethtranslationapp@outlook.com
